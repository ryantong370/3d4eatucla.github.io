About Us

3D Printing at UCLA



3D Printing is a combination of art, engineering, and problem-solving. At 3D4E, we strive to inspire people through the power of computer-aided design, and leave our own mark by building practical solutions to real-world problems through 3D-printed products.

Our Projects



Our clubâ€™s main focus is on group projects, in which we design, manufacture, and test using CAD software and 3D printing. These projects range from recreational, like RC cars, to life-changing, like our prosthetic hand. In addition to our group efforts, 3D4E also supports members with the resources and expertise they might need to pursue their own individual ideas.

Project Hours: 7PM - 8PM Thursdays; Boelter Library SEL Learning Commons


Workshops



Regardless of your background, major, or design experience, we are committed to teaching all our general members how to 3D Print from the ground up. During the Fall Quarter, after we recruit new members, we will host weekly workshops to train everyone how to use CAD tools like Fusion360, and translate your designs into manufacturable prints.

Workshop Hours: 6PM - 7PM Thursdays; Boelter Library SEL Learning Commons
